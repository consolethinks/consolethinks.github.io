Desert Circle - Map by the-middleman

Mission: Conquer the castle and kill the King

DO NOT ATTACK DIRECTLY - TOO DANGEROUS

1. Conquer the outposts in the desert.
2. Use the mortar to breach the castle walls.
3. Enter castle through the breach
4. Destroy the explosive crates to destroy the
   sniper towers
5. Open castle gates
6. Conquer castle
7. Kill the King



Feel free to use all textures, models, sounds etc.
that come with this map. Credits are nice but not
mandatory.
